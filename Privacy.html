Introduction and Overview


This Privacy and Cookies policy applies to 'https://olgaamaya.netlify.app/'. By using this website various pieces of personal data will be collected. Personal information is any data by which you could be personally identified. The privacy policy clarifies what information I collect and what I use it for. It is also a explanation for what reason and how this is happening. As with any privacy and cookies policy, I recommend you have a read through this. I am Leon Landry. I am a architecture student and operator of this page.

How do I collect your data?


Some data are collected when you visit this website. For example data you enter on a contact form. Other data are collected by the IT systems when you enter my website. These data are technical data such as the operating system and browser you are using or data about when you accessed. These data are collected automatically as soon as you visit my website.


What do I use your data for?


I do not share your data and believe in collecting only the information that is absolutely necessary. Part of the data is saved to the general functioning of the site. Further data can be used to analyze how visitors interact with the page.


How long do I collect your data?


For any information you send to me via the contact form which is stored in my email inbox, I will keep for a maximum of 3 months following the end of our communications. In case you want me to remove the information within a shorter timescale sent me a request to the contact notice.


What rights do you have respecting your data?


You always have the right to request information about your stored data and that it be corrected, blocked, or deleted. You can contact me at any time using the address given in the contact notice if you have more questions about the issue of your data privacy.
